Welcome to Catebi’s website!

Have you seen some cats missing the tip of one ear?

The reason they are missing the tip of one ear is that it indicates that they have been surgically neutered... So far, hundreds of cats have been spayed in Tbilisi and we intend to do more.

Who are we?

Catebi is a group of volunteers in Tbilisi... Thanks to us, more than 200 cats have been sterilized in Tbilisi, and we continue to do so.

Why neuter the cats?

Firstly, neutering helps control the animal population... Remember: in 6 years, a pair of cats and their offspring can produce up to 420,000 kittens.
